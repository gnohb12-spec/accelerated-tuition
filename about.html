Who we are

Great service begins and ends with great people.

Over the years, we've learned that great service begins and ends with experienced and friendly professionals — which explains our rigorous hiring process. We believe our team is the best in the business, and we have complete and total confidence in every person providing our services.

Accelerated Tuition pushes intelligent students to their full potential at the highest level of education. With a focus on personalised service, competitive rates, and genuine care, we're always striving to exceed expectations and bring out the best in our students.

Why are we able to provide such incredible value to our students? Because we focus our efforts on years 11 and 12 Maths only. These are the years that count, so we've dedicated our business to making sure our students absolutely ace it when they need help the most.
